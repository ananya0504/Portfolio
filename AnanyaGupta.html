About Me

Hi there, welcome to my portfolio! My name is Ananya, and I am a tenacious leader with a diverse skill set. I have a vision that inspires. I work for and strive for improvising the quality of life with technological advancements and uplift the community with my talents. I am a final-year Computer Science undergrad from Banasthali Vidyapith. I am a learner and I love making apps and designing things, be it databases or UI.


I'm very passionate when it comes to learning new things and making new projects. I love collaborating! I have taken part in various hackathons and also stood among the Top 10 final teams in HackOverflow 3.0, organised by Chandigarh University. I have been a part of various programs like #100DaysWireUs challenge and #21DaysOfFlutter.


I am serving as the Multimedia Director at the Rotaract Club of Delhi Femina, where I am responsible for guiding and managing a team of 9 members in poster designing, graphic designing and social media handling. I love contributing and giving back to the community from which I have learnt from and to do that, I have also initiated my own LeanIn circle for my university with an aim to help students to get started with open source, learn various technologies and upskill every day.
